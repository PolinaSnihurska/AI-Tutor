# Requirements Document

## Introduction

The AI Tutoring Platform is an intelligent educational ecosystem that combines personalized learning, automatic content generation, adaptive explanations, and detailed analytics. The platform aims to replace traditional tutoring in many tasks by explaining topics, creating learning plans, generating tests, analyzing mistakes, and tracking progress. It serves as a centralized solution for students preparing for exams and parents monitoring their children's education.

## Glossary

- **AI Tutor**: The artificial intelligence system that provides explanations, generates content, and adapts to student needs
- **Learning Plan**: A personalized daily schedule of educational tasks generated by the system
- **Test Module**: The component responsible for generating, administering, and evaluating assessments
- **Analytics Dashboard**: The interface displaying student progress, performance metrics, and predictions
- **Parent Cabinet**: A dedicated interface for parents to monitor their children's learning activities
- **Student Cabinet**: The personal workspace where students access lessons, materials, and achievements
- **Premium Tier**: The paid subscription level offering unlimited access to all platform features
- **Free Tier**: The limited access level providing basic platform functionality
- **Heatmap**: A visual representation showing areas where students make the most mistakes
- **Adaptive Questions**: Test questions that adjust difficulty based on student performance
- **MAU**: Monthly Active Users
- **DAU**: Daily Active Users
- **NMT**: National Multi-subject Test (Ukrainian standardized exam)

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: AI-Assisted Learning

**User Story:** As a student, I want the AI to explain school subjects in simple terms, so that I can better understand complex topics.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student requests an explanation for a topic, THE AI Tutor SHALL provide a response within 2 seconds
2. THE AI Tutor SHALL adapt the complexity of explanations based on the student's age and comprehension level
3. WHEN providing explanations, THE AI Tutor SHALL include examples and step-by-step solutions
4. WHERE a student is using the Free Tier, THE AI Tutor SHALL limit responses to 5 explanations per day
5. WHERE a student is using the Premium Tier, THE AI Tutor SHALL provide unlimited explanations

### Requirement 2: Personalized Learning Plans

**User Story:** As a student preparing for exams, I want a personalized learning plan, so that I know what to study each day.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student completes the initial assessment, THE Learning Plan SHALL generate a personalized schedule within 5 seconds
2. THE Learning Plan SHALL include daily tasks based on the student's knowledge gaps and exam timeline
3. THE Learning Plan SHALL send intelligent reminders to students about upcoming tasks
4. WHERE a student is using the Free Tier, THE Learning Plan SHALL provide access to 60% of theoretical materials
5. WHERE a student is using the Premium Tier, THE Learning Plan SHALL provide access to 100% of materials and extended planning features

### Requirement 3: Test Generation and Assessment

**User Story:** As a student, I want to take practice tests with detailed feedback, so that I can identify and fix my mistakes.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student requests a test, THE Test Module SHALL generate the test within 1 second
2. THE Test Module SHALL provide tests at varying difficulty levels based on student performance
3. WHEN a student completes a test, THE Test Module SHALL provide detailed explanations for incorrect answers
4. THE Test Module SHALL implement adaptive questioning that adjusts difficulty based on responses
5. WHERE a student is using the Free Tier, THE Test Module SHALL limit access to 3 tests per day
6. WHERE a student is using the Premium Tier, THE Test Module SHALL provide unlimited test generation

### Requirement 4: Progress Analytics

**User Story:** As a student, I want to see my progress over time, so that I know where I'm making mistakes and improving.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Analytics Dashboard SHALL display a performance graph showing the student's success rate over time
2. THE Analytics Dashboard SHALL generate a heatmap highlighting topics where the student makes the most errors
3. THE Analytics Dashboard SHALL provide success predictions with a minimum accuracy of 85%
4. THE Analytics Dashboard SHALL update performance metrics every 10 seconds during active learning sessions
5. WHERE a student is using the Free Tier, THE Analytics Dashboard SHALL provide basic analytics only
6. WHERE a student is using the Premium Tier, THE Analytics Dashboard SHALL provide comprehensive analytics including predictive models

### Requirement 5: Parent Monitoring

**User Story:** As a parent, I want to monitor my child's learning progress, so that I can support them effectively.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Parent Cabinet SHALL display the child's learning statistics for selected time periods
2. THE Parent Cabinet SHALL highlight weak topics requiring additional attention
3. THE Parent Cabinet SHALL provide recommendations for supporting the child's learning
4. THE Parent Cabinet SHALL allow parents to control and monitor the child's learning time
5. WHERE a parent has a Premium Tier subscription, THE Parent Cabinet SHALL support monitoring up to 3 children

### Requirement 6: Student Personal Workspace

**User Story:** As a student, I want a personal workspace to access my lessons and track achievements, so that I stay organized.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Student Cabinet SHALL maintain a history of completed lessons and activities
2. THE Student Cabinet SHALL allow students to save and bookmark materials for later review
3. THE Student Cabinet SHALL display favorite topics selected by the student
4. THE Student Cabinet SHALL track and display student achievements and milestones
5. THE Student Cabinet SHALL provide quick access to recently studied materials

### Requirement 7: System Performance

**User Story:** As a user, I want the platform to respond quickly, so that my learning experience is smooth and uninterrupted.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE AI Tutor SHALL respond to queries within 2 seconds under normal load conditions
2. THE Test Module SHALL generate tests within 1 second of request
3. THE Analytics Dashboard SHALL update metrics within 10 seconds during active sessions
4. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL maintain 99.9% server uptime
5. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL maintain a crash rate below 0.1%

### Requirement 8: Cross-Platform Compatibility

**User Story:** As a user, I want to access the platform from different devices and browsers, so that I can learn anywhere.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL support Chrome, Firefox, and Edge browsers
2. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL provide desktop applications for Windows and macOS
3. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL implement responsive design for different screen sizes
4. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL maintain consistent functionality across all supported platforms
5. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL synchronize user data across devices in real-time

### Requirement 9: Data Security and Privacy

**User Story:** As a parent, I want my child's data to be secure, so that I can trust the platform with sensitive information.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL encrypt all user data both in transit and at rest
2. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL comply with GDPR regulations for data protection
3. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL implement parental controls for student accounts
4. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL allow users to export or delete their data upon request
5. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

### Requirement 10: User Experience

**User Story:** As a student, I want an intuitive and distraction-free interface, so that I can focus on learning.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL implement a minimalist educational design aesthetic
2. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL provide a "concentration mode" that removes distractions
3. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL adapt the interface based on user preferences and accessibility needs
4. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL provide clear navigation between all major features
5. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL load all pages within 3 seconds on standard internet connections

### Requirement 11: Subscription Management

**User Story:** As a user, I want to easily manage my subscription, so that I can upgrade or cancel as needed.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL provide clear differentiation between Free Tier and Premium Tier features
2. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL allow users to upgrade from Free Tier to Premium Tier seamlessly
3. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL support family plans for up to 3 children under Premium Tier
4. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL process subscription payments securely through established payment providers
5. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL allow users to cancel subscriptions with immediate effect or at period end

### Requirement 12: Content Quality

**User Story:** As a student, I want accurate and high-quality educational content, so that I learn correctly.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE AI Tutor SHALL provide explanations that are factually accurate and curriculum-aligned
2. THE Test Module SHALL generate questions that match official exam formats and difficulty levels
3. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL implement quality filters to validate AI-generated content
4. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL allow students to report incorrect or unclear content
5. THE AI Tutoring Platform SHALL continuously improve content quality through model training and feedback
