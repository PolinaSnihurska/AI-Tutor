## Production Notes

### Visual Style
- Clean, modern interface recordings
- Smooth transitions and animations
- Highlight cursor movements and clicks
- Use callouts for important UI elements
- Brand colors: Blue (#667eea) and Purple (#764ba2)

### Audio
- Professional voiceover (friendly, conversational tone)
- Background music (upbeat but not distracting, -20dB)
- Sound effects for interactions (subtle)
- Clear audio quality (no background noise)

### Text Overlays
- Large, readable fonts
- High contrast
- Appear with smooth animations
- Stay on screen long enough to read (3-5 seconds)

### Pacing
- Not too fast (viewers need time to absorb)
- Not too slow (maintain engagement)
- Pause between major sections
- Use music to guide pacing

### Accessibility
- Include closed captions
- High contrast visuals
- Clear, simple language
- Avoid flashing or rapid movements

### Distribution
- **Main Demo**: Landing page, YouTube, email campaigns
- **Student Journey**: Social media, ads
- **Feature Spotlights**: Instagram, TikTok, Twitter
- **Parent Video**: Facebook, parent-focused ads

### Formats
- **Landscape (16:9)**: YouTube, landing page, email
- **Square (1:1)**: Instagram feed, Facebook
- **Vertical (9:16)**: Instagram Stories, TikTok, Reels

### Thumbnails
Create eye-catching thumbnails with:
- Platform screenshot
- Bold text overlay
- Bright colors
- Clear value proposition
