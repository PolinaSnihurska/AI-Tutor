## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the platform homepage and click **Sign Up**
2. Choose **Student** as your account type
3. Enter your email, create a password, and fill in your profile information
4. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
5. Complete the onboarding wizard to set up your learning preferences

### Setting Up Your Profile

During onboarding, you'll be asked to:
- Select your grade level
- Choose subjects you want to study
- Set your exam target (if applicable)
- Select your subscription tier (Free or Premium)

## AI Chat & Explanations

### Asking Questions

1. Click **Ask AI** or navigate to the Chat page
2. Type your question in natural language
   - Example: "Explain photosynthesis in simple terms"
   - Example: "How do I solve quadratic equations?"
3. The AI will respond within 2 seconds with:
   - Clear explanation adapted to your level
   - Examples and step-by-step solutions
   - Related topics you might want to explore

### Tips for Better Explanations

- **Be specific**: "Explain the Pythagorean theorem" is better than "math help"
- **Ask follow-ups**: If something is unclear, ask the AI to explain differently
- **Request examples**: Say "Can you give me an example?" for practical demonstrations
- **Adjust complexity**: Ask the AI to "explain like I'm 10" or "explain in more detail"

### Conversation History

All your conversations are saved. Access them from:
- The Chat page sidebar
- Your Student Cabinet under "Lesson History"

### Usage Limits

- **Free Tier**: 5 AI explanations per day
- **Premium Tier**: Unlimited explanations

## Taking Tests

### Generating a Test

1. Navigate to **Tests** from the main menu
2. Choose from:
   - **Quick Test**: AI generates a test based on your recent topics
   - **Custom Test**: Select specific subjects, topics, and difficulty
   - **Practice Exam**: Full-length exam simulation

3. Configure your test:
   - Subject(s)
   - Topics to cover
   - Number of questions
   - Difficulty level
   - Time limit (optional)

4. Click **Generate Test** (takes ~1 second)

### Taking a Test

1. Read each question carefully
2. Select your answer for multiple choice questions
3. Type your response for open-ended questions
4. Use the navigation buttons to move between questions
5. The timer (if enabled) shows remaining time
6. Click **Submit Test** when finished

### Test Features

- **Save Progress**: Tests auto-save, so you can pause and resume
- **Flag Questions**: Mark questions to review later
- **Calculator**: Available for math tests
- **Scratch Pad**: Take notes during the test

### Reviewing Results

After submission, you'll see:
- **Overall Score**: Your percentage and grade
- **Question-by-Question Review**: See what you got right/wrong
- **Detailed Explanations**: Learn from your mistakes
- **Weak Topics**: Areas that need more practice
- **Recommendations**: Suggested next steps

## Tips for Success

### Study Strategies

1. **Consistency is Key**: Study a little every day rather than cramming
2. **Follow Your Plan**: Trust the AI-generated learning plan
3. **Test Regularly**: Take practice tests to identify weak areas
4. **Review Mistakes**: Always review test explanations
5. **Ask Questions**: Use the AI chat whenever you're confused

### Using the Platform Effectively

1. **Set a Schedule**: Dedicate specific times for studying
2. **Use Concentration Mode**: Enable in settings to remove distractions
3. **Track Your Progress**: Check analytics weekly to stay motivated
4. **Adjust Your Plan**: Update your exam date and goals as needed
5. **Take Breaks**: The platform will remind you to rest

### Maximizing AI Explanations

1. **Start Broad, Then Narrow**: Ask general questions first, then dive deeper
2. **Request Different Formats**: Ask for explanations, examples, or practice problems
3. **Build on Previous Conversations**: The AI remembers your chat history
4. **Report Issues**: If an explanation is wrong, report it to help improve the system

### Preparing for Exams

1. **Set Your Exam Date**: This helps the platform create an optimal study plan
2. **Take Practice Exams**: Simulate real test conditions
3. **Focus on Weak Topics**: Use the heatmap to identify areas needing work
4. **Review Predictions**: Check what factors are affecting your predicted score
5. **Stay Consistent**: Maintain your study streak leading up to the exam
