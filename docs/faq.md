# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is the AI Tutoring Platform?

The AI Tutoring Platform is an intelligent educational system that provides personalized learning experiences through AI-powered explanations, adaptive testing, customized learning plans, and detailed progress analytics. It's designed to help students prepare for exams and support parents in monitoring their children's education.

### Who is this platform for?

- **Students** preparing for exams (especially standardized tests like NMT)
- **Parents** who want to monitor and support their children's learning
- **Learners** of all ages seeking personalized educational support

### What subjects are covered?

The platform covers all major academic subjects including:
- Mathematics
- Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
- Languages (native and foreign)
- History
- Geography
- And more

### How does the AI work?

Our AI uses advanced language models (GPT-4 and similar) to:
- Understand your questions in natural language
- Generate explanations adapted to your level
- Create personalized tests and learning plans
- Analyze your performance and predict outcomes
- Provide recommendations for improvement

## Account & Access

### How do I create an account?

1. Visit the homepage and click "Sign Up"
2. Choose your account type (Student or Parent)
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete the onboarding process

### Can I have both a student and parent account?

Yes! You can create separate accounts for different roles. However, each email address can only be associated with one account.

### How do I link my child's account to my parent account?

From your parent dashboard:
1. Go to Settings → Children
2. Click "Link Existing Child" or "Add New Child"
3. Follow the prompts to connect accounts
4. Your child will need to approve the link (if they have an existing account)

### I forgot my password. What do I do?

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with your new password

### Can I use the platform on multiple devices?

Yes! Your account syncs across all devices in real-time. You can access the platform from:
- Web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
- Desktop apps (Windows, macOS)
- Mobile browsers (responsive design)

## Learning Features

### How accurate are the AI explanations?

Our AI provides highly accurate, curriculum-aligned explanations. However:
- Always verify critical information with textbooks or teachers
- Report any errors using the "Report Issue" button
- We continuously improve accuracy through user feedback

### Can the AI help with homework?

Yes! The AI can:
- Explain concepts you don't understand
- Provide step-by-step solutions
- Offer practice problems
- Help you learn the material

However, we encourage using the AI to *learn*, not just to get answers.

### How are learning plans created?

Learning plans are personalized based on:
- Your initial assessment results
- Your exam date and goals
- Your performance on tests
- Your study patterns and consistency
- Knowledge gaps identified by the AI

The plan updates automatically as you progress.

### How does adaptive testing work?

Adaptive tests adjust difficulty in real-time:
- Answer correctly → get harder questions
- Answer incorrectly → get easier questions or review material
- This identifies your true skill level efficiently
- Helps focus practice on appropriate difficulty

### Are the practice tests like real exams?

Yes! Our tests:
- Match official exam formats
- Cover curriculum-aligned content
- Include similar question types
- Simulate real test conditions
- Provide timing practice

### How accurate are the success predictions?

Our predictions are 85%+ accurate based on:
- Your current performance trends
- Study consistency
- Time remaining until exam
- Historical data from thousands of students

Use predictions as guidance, not guarantees.

## Progress & Analytics

### How is my performance score calculated?

Your overall performance score (0-100) is calculated from:
- Test scores (weighted by difficulty): 50%
- Learning plan completion: 20%
- Study consistency: 15%
- Improvement rate: 15%

### What does the heatmap show?

The heatmap visualizes your error patterns:
- **Red**: Topics with high error rates (need practice)
- **Yellow**: Topics with moderate errors
- **Green**: Topics you've mastered

Click any topic for detailed statistics and practice recommendations.

### How often does my progress update?

- Real-time during active learning sessions (every 10 seconds)
- Daily snapshots for historical tracking
- Weekly summaries for trend analysis

### Can I export my progress data?

Yes! Go to Settings → Privacy → Export Data to download:
- All test results
- Study history
- Progress analytics
- Learning plan history
- AI conversation history

## Parent Cabinet

### What can I see in the Parent Cabinet?

You can monitor:
- Your child's study time and patterns
- Test scores and performance trends
- Weak topics and areas needing attention
- Learning plan progress
- Activity logs
- Success predictions

### Can my child see that I'm monitoring them?

Yes, transparency is important. Your child will know:
- That their account is linked to yours
- What level of monitoring you've enabled
- When you view their cabinet (optional notification)

### How much control do I have over my child's account?

You can:
- Set study time limits
- Control content access
- View activity logs
- Receive performance alerts
- Adjust notification preferences

You cannot:
- Log in as your child
- Change their password without their knowledge
- Delete their work without permission

### Can I monitor multiple children?

Yes! Premium family plans support up to 3 children. You can:
- Switch between children easily
- View individual or family dashboards
- Set different controls for each child
- Receive consolidated notifications

### At what age should I stop monitoring?

This depends on your child's maturity and responsibility. We recommend:
- **Ages 6-11**: High monitoring
- **Ages 12-14**: Moderate monitoring
- **Ages 15-18**: Light monitoring, focus on support

Gradually reduce monitoring as your child demonstrates responsibility.

## Technical Issues

### The platform is loading slowly. What can I do?

Try these steps:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Disable browser extensions
5. Restart your device

If issues persist, contact support.

### I'm not receiving email notifications.

Check these:
1. Verify your email address in Settings
2. Check your spam/junk folder
3. Add noreply@aitutoring.com to your contacts
4. Verify notification settings are enabled
5. Try a different email address

### The AI isn't responding to my questions.

Possible causes:
1. You've reached your daily limit (Free tier)
2. Temporary service issue
3. Question contains inappropriate content
4. Network connectivity problem

Try refreshing the page or contact support if the issue persists.

### My test results aren't saving.

Ensure:
1. You have a stable internet connection
2. You clicked "Submit Test" (not just closed the page)
3. Your browser allows cookies
4. You're logged in to your account

Tests auto-save progress, but must be submitted to record results.

### I can't log in to my account.

Try:
1. Verify you're using the correct email
2. Check if Caps Lock is on
3. Reset your password
4. Clear browser cookies
5. Try a different browser

If you still can't log in, contact support with your email address.
