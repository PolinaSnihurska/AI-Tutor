## User Interview Guide

### Introduction (5 minutes)
- Thank participant
- Explain interview purpose
- Get consent for recording
- Set expectations (30 minutes)

### Background (5 minutes)
1. Tell me about your experience with online learning tools
2. What are your main goals for exam preparation?
3. How do you currently study?

### Platform Experience (15 minutes)
4. Walk me through how you typically use the platform
5. What's your favorite feature? Why?
6. What's your least favorite feature? Why?
7. Tell me about a time something didn't work as expected
8. What would make the platform more valuable to you?

### Specific Features (5 minutes)
9. [Ask about specific features based on their usage]
10. [Probe on any interesting feedback from surveys]

### Wrap-up (5 minutes)
11. If you could change one thing, what would it be?
12. Would you recommend this to a friend? Why or why not?
13. Any final thoughts or suggestions?
14. Thank you and next steps

---

**Usage Instructions:**

1. **Weekly Surveys**: Send every Friday via email
2. **End-of-Beta Survey**: Send in final week of beta
3. **Bug Reports**: Available in-app and via email
4. **Feature Requests**: Collected through in-app widget
5. **Interviews**: Schedule with 10-15 participants per phase

**Data Collection:**
- Use Google Forms or Typeform for surveys
- Store responses in spreadsheet for analysis
- Tag and categorize feedback
- Share insights with team weekly
