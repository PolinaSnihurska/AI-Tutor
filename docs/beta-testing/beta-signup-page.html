Frequently Asked Questions

When will I get access?

We're accepting beta testers in waves. You'll receive an email invitation within 2-4 weeks of signing up, depending on which phase you're selected for.

Is the beta really free?

Yes! Beta testers get free premium access during the beta period and for 6 months after launch. No credit card required.

What's expected of beta testers?

We ask that you use the platform regularly, report any bugs you find, and complete weekly feedback surveys. User interviews are optional but appreciated.

Can I invite friends?

Once you're accepted into the beta, you'll receive referral codes to invite friends and family.

What if I find a bug?

Great! That's what beta testing is for. You can report bugs directly in the app or email us at beta@aitutoring.com.

Will my data be safe?

Absolutely. We use the same security measures as our production environment, including encryption and GDPR compliance.
